About Justin

Find out about the real person behind the web developer.

Web development has been beckoning me for close to a decade, since as a teacher, I created websites, blogs and online activities for my own students to use. With so many negative changes in the education field over the past decade, I was inspired to seek a new career in web development and decided last year that my calling was in a field where I could battle it out with a browser, jab at JavaScript and seek my success with CSS. I love the challenge of taking a design and bringing it to life on the web with a pixel-perfect rendition!

Since my decision to enter the field of web development, I have spent nearly every minute of my free time studying and learning the nuances and modern languages of building websites and web apps today. All this while teaching full-time, caring for a 2-year old son and awaiting a new baby. This path I have taken illustrates how motivated and dedicated I am to work and continue learning in this field for the rest of my career.

Why hire me? I believe that my reputation rests on my results. Whether its in a classroom or on the web, I expect excellence and work until I achieve it. My reputation is built not just from my clients' satisfaction but my own. I create attractive, efficient, accessible, websites that ultimately provide users with what they need and clients with the unsurpassed quality they expect.

I have been specializing in Front-End Web Development while learning other aspects of design and back-end development to enable me to understand the big picture. I can provide the design functionality you need to reach your web visitors on an array of different devices that can be easily adapted in the future. I enjoy many types of projects for clients including small business, advertising, governments, and non-profit organizations.

My Offline Life

I may not have much free time after after work, studying web development, taking care of my two-year old son and my wonderful wife; but if I do you will often find me playing cards and games like Settlers of Catan. Or perhaps enjoying an afternoon of canoeing after a night of good old tent camping. If its a Sunday I'll be agonizing over another Detroit Lions' game and quite likely looking forward to the Red Wings' hockey season. As a Political Science major, I diligently do my daily reading of political happenings and international events. My office staff includes two dogs and two cats who are generally lazy buffoons.

Every chance we get my family loves to travel the world. We love the sights and cultures of our world and all it has to discover. I have worked at the United Nations and lived for a year in the Czech Republic as a US Fulbright Scholar. I enjoy meeting and working with people from all cultures and walks of life. I bring a positive and easy-going outlook to life and work and love my new career in web development.
